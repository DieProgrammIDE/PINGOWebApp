STORY:
======
The PINGO software originated from an "eClickr" software developed by **Michael Whittaker** for a project paper.
Together with **Juergen Neumann** we polished the software. In mid-2013 **Christoph Bach** joined our team.
We develop at the Chair for Information Management & E-Finance at the University of Paderborn (www.upb.de/winfo2).
